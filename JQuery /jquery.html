How to Order Food From Your Smartphone

Step 1

Download Postmates from the APP store

Step 2

Browse the categories and decide what type of food you are in the mood for

Step 3

Chose a resturant and read through the menu

Step 4

Click on all the items you want, add them to the cart, and then hit the checkout button

Step 5

If this is your first time using the app you will have to enter your credit card information, all other times it will be saved

Step 6

Click "Get it Now" "and your order will show up at your current location within an hour
