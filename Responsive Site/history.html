Sigma Delta Tau History

  • Nationally founded at Cornell Univeristy on March 25th, 1917
  • The national philanthropy is PCAA (Prevent Child Abuse America)
  • The founding sisters are Dora Bloom Turteltaub, Amy Apfel Tishman, Marian Gerber Greenberg, Grace Srenco Grossman, Inez Dane Ross, Regene Freund Cohane, and Lenore Rubinow
  • The original name was Sigma Delta Phi, but the founders soon found out that name was already taken by another greek organization

University of Miami Chapter

  • Alpha Mu Chapter
  • Founded on October 26th, 1957
  • This chapter was selected as one of nine chapters to be honored with the Diamond Chapter Award in 2016
  • Includes a number of girls from very diverse backgrounds who are involved in activities all over Miami
